What is Realize?

Our Vision

To be cliche about it, we want to make the world a better place. We have seen the power of self-monitoring firsthand, and the powerful ways that it can help improve health. We see the adoption of wearable devices and mobile health apps exploding.

What we don't see is a clear, unified, and secure way to pull data together and gain actionable insight. We believe that people should be able to own their data, especially when it is this sensitive.

The collaborative and open source nature of Realize is a product of this vision. We hope you enjoy using it as much as we enjoy making it. If you ever want to peek at the code, check it out here.
